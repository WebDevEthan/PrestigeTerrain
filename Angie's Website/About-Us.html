The Husband Wife Duo

Hi! I'm Angela!

Our Commitments

At Prestige Terrain, we are a husband-wife duo and veteran-owned firm specializing in providing layered services and solutions in civil/structural engineering, land use planning, GIS mapping, residential site plans and residential building administration. Our commitment is to provide our clients with experiences that are responsive, streamlined, and timely with integrity at the core.

My Dreams

At the height of Covid in 2020, we moved our family from Oregon to Michigan to be close to family. We built our house during that time, and we enjoyed the experience, so I decided to get my residential builders license and start building homes at some point. In the meantime, this business was started so that we can collectively use our experiences and professional training to provide excellent services to those that need them. This business is also a way for me to be a stay-at-home mom and provide an income for our family. Our end goal is to hand our children an investment for their future.
